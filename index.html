Meet Kelsey

Kelsey Ferrell is a multitalented, award winning performer based out of Santa Cruz/Berkeley. She creates music under the artist name Feral, and her debut album Trauma Portfolio is out now on Spotify, Apple Music, and all other major streaming services. Kelsey also performs stand up comedy. At UC Berkeley, she was editor in chief of satire publication The Free Peach . An alum of UC Berkeley's baking decal, she also enjoys baking sweet treats. In her free time she can be found making memes and shopping for vintage Y2K clothing.
